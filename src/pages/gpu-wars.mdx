# GPU Wars

## Competition That Drove Innovation

GPU development was heavily shaped by competition between major hardware companies.

---

## Key Rivalries

- 3dfx vs NVIDIA (early 3D graphics era)
- NVIDIA vs ATI/AMD (shader and GPU standardization era)
- NVIDIA vs AMD vs Intel (AI computing era)

---

## Key Idea

Competition accelerated innovation in GPU architecture and performance.
