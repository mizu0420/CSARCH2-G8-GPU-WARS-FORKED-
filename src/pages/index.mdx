# GPU Wars

## Five Turning Points That Changed Computing

*Explore the remarkable evolution of the Graphics Processing Unit - from a specialized graphics accelerator to one of the most influential technologies driving modern computing.*

---

## Welcome to the Exhibit!

Today, GPUs power everything, from immersive video games and realistic graphics all the way to artificial intelligence, scientific research, and large-scale data processing. Originally created to accelerate graphics rendering, the GPU has evolved into a cornerstone of modern computer architecture.

**Discover how five pivotal moments transformed the GPU into the engine behind today's most demanding computing workloads!**

## What You'll Explore

- **Introduction** - Discover what a GPU is and why it became essential to computing.
- **Before GPUs** - Learn how CPUs handled graphics before dedicated graphics hardware existed and why this approach reached its limits.
- **Five Turning Points** - Follow the milestones that changed GPU history.
- **Architecture Evolution** - See how GPU hardware evolved from fixed-function graphics to massively parallel computing.
- **GPU Wars** - Explore how competition between manufacturers accelerated innovation.
- **The AI Computing Era** - Learn how GPUs became the foundation of modern artificial intelligence.

---

This exhibit features an **interactive timeline** that allows visitors to explore the defining milestones in GPU history - from the first consumer 3D accelerators in the 1990s to today's AI supercomputing platforms.
