# Conclusion

## The GPU as a Foundation of Modern Computing

The GPU has evolved from a simple graphics accelerator into one of the most important computing platforms in modern technology.

---

## Impact

- Enables modern gaming
- Powers AI and machine learning
- Accelerates scientific simulations
- Supports modern data centers

---

## Final Thought

The GPU did not just improve graphics—it transformed computing itself.
