# GPU Wars: Five Turning Points That Changed Computing

## Museum Entrance

*Welcome to the virtual exhibit exploring the remarkable evolution of the Graphics Processing Unit (GPU)—from a specialized graphics accelerator to one of the most influential computing technologies of the modern era.*

---

## Welcome

The **GPU Wars Virtual Exhibit** invites you to explore one of the most significant transformations in computer architecture! What began as dedicated hardware for rendering graphics has evolved into a massively parallel computing platform that powers artificial intelligence, scientific research, high-performance computing, and modern interactive experiences.

This exhibit is organized as a series of galleries. Each section highlights a different stage in the GPU's evolution, allowing you to explore at your own pace and discover how technological innovation and industry competition reshaped modern computing, though following the recommended sequence provides a deeper understanding of how GPU technology evolved over time.

---

## Explore the Exhibit

Your journey through the museum is divided into six galleries:

- **[Introduction: Rise of GPUs](/introduction)**
  Learn what GPUs are and why they became essential to modern computing.

- **[Before GPUs](/before-gpus)**
  Discover how CPUs handled graphics before dedicated graphics hardware existed and why new solutions became necessary.

- **[Five Turning Points](/turning-points)**
  Explore the pivotal milestones that transformed graphics accelerators into powerful computing platforms.

- **[GPU Architecture Evolution](/architecture)**
  Follow the progression of GPU design from fixed-function hardware to programmable and AI-accelerated architectures.

- **[GPU Wars](/gpu-wars)**
  See how competition between leading manufacturers drove innovation across multiple generations of GPU technology.

- **[Conclusion & Legacy](/conclusion)**
  Reflect on the GPU's lasting impact on gaming, scientific computing, and artificial intelligence.
